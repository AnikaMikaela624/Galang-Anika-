Hi my name is Anika Galang

I never really liked any sports when I was young but when my parents enrolled me for swimming I got really nervous because I didn’t know how to. My first training came and it was really fun. After that I fell inlove with swimming. I didn’t continue it to advance because it was expensive. When I heard that there was sports training in Perpetual I instantly enrolled. Because of that I eventually joined my very first competition in UP diliman and I was really excited. The competition ended and I went home happy because I went home with 4 medals in total in all events.

The medals I went home with

  • 25 Meter butterfly – silver medal🥈
  • 25 Meter backstroke – bronze medal🥉
  • 25 Meter breaststroke – silver medal🥈
  • 25 Meter freestyle – gold medal🥇
